About

Welcome to Lo-Fi Music Hub — your cozy corner on the web for chill beats and relaxing vibes.

This project was created as part of my web development portfolio, combining my passion for design, music, and frontend development.

Here, you can explore a curated selection of lo-fi playlists perfect for studying, working, or simply unwinding after a long day. Every element of this site was carefully crafted to evoke calmness, creativity, and simplicity — the core of the lo-fi aesthetic.

Although this is a fictional project, it represents my commitment to creating beautiful, functional, and meaningful digital experiences.

Thanks for stopping by!
